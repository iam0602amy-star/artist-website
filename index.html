FAQ

How much do tattoos cost?

Pricing depends on size and detail. Hourly rates are shared after consultation.

Where are you located?

Location varies based on guest spots and studio availability. Please feel free to reach out through email/dm me on instagram.

How should I prepare?

Eat well, stay hydrated, sleep well, and avoid alcohol before your appointment.

Do you offer touch-ups and what is the cost?

Complimentary touch-ups are available within the first 12 months.

How do I know if my tattoo is healing correctly?

Normal healing includes slight redness, itchiness and peeling; Please contact the studio immediately if you have concerns, or seek medical attention if you experience excessive swelling or signs of infection.

What should I wear for the appointment?

Please wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed.
